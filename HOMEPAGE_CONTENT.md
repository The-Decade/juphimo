**Our 5-Step Screening Process:**

1. **Initial Application Review:** Verification of credentials, work history, and qualifications
2. **Background Checks:** Criminal record verification, identity confirmation, and address validation
3. **Skills Assessment:** Practical tests and competency evaluations specific to their role
4. **Reference Verification:** Direct contact with previous employers to verify performance and reliability
5. **Personal Interview:** Face-to-face assessment of professionalism, communication, and cultural fit

**Additional Safeguards:**

- Professional reference letters
